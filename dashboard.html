Sadie

Breed: Pitbull
Age: 2yrs

Sadie is a very sweet girl with a great temperament. Her previous owner has sadly passed and so she is in need of a loving home. We are told by relatives of her previous owner that Sadie is great with children of any age and loves to go on walks in the park. Her favorite game is Tug-of-War and she comes with a basket of stuffed toys from her previous home. If you're interested in giving this good girl a home, please contact us!

Lobo

Breed: Alaskan Husky
Age: 1yr

Lobo's story is a comeback for the ages. We found this poor guy in on the side of the highway after a concerned motorist called us to report an abandoned pup. Tragically, when our retrieval team arrived on the scene, Lobo had already been struck by a passing vehicle. Our driver rushed him to the animal hospital and, despite multiple fractures, Lobo made a full recovery! He now sprints at full speed across our play yard with his new friends. We would love to see this growing boy go to a forever home with a person or people who can give him the attention and love he needs.
